Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a delicious treat; however, it is important to use the right recipe. This recipe from Serious Eats is one of my favourites!

Ingredients

  • 225g unsalted butter
  • 1 ice cube
  • 280g all-purpose flour
  • 3g baking soda
  • 4g Diamond Crystal kosher salt
  • 140g granulated sugar
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 10ml vanilla extract
  • 140g dark brown sugar
  • 225g semisweet chocolate, roughly chopped
  • coarse sea salt, for garnish

Steps

  1. Melt butter in medium saucepan over medium-high heat until golden brown.
  2. Remove melted butter from heat and transfer to bowl. Whisk in ice cube.
  3. Place butter bowl in fridge to cool completely. About 20 minutes.
  4. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  5. Place granulated sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with whisk attachment. Whisk on medium-high speed until mixture is pale. About 5 minutes.
  6. Fit paddle attachment to mixer. When brown butter mixture has cooled add it to the stand mixer. Add brown sugar and egg to stand mixer. Mix on medium for 15 seconds.
  7. Add flour mixture and mix on low speed until barely combined. About 15 seconds.
  8. Add chocolate and mix on low speed for 15 seconds.
  9. Transfer to airtight container and refrigerate overnight and up to 3 days.
  10. When ready to bake. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  11. Place 1-ounce balls of dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  12. Bake for 13 to 16 minutes. Cookies should be golden brown around edges but still soft.
  13. Garnish with sea salt.

Enjoy!
