Pizza

Description

Pizza is a delicious weekend meal. It is so easy to order online, but, surprisingly, even easier to make at home with a bit of foresight and planning.

Ingredients

  • 800g 00-tipo flour
  • 512g water
  • 4g active dry yeast
  • 16g fine sea salt
  • 28oz can peeled whole San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 gloves peeled garlic
  • mozzarella, to your chosen quantity
  • fresh or dry basil

Steps

  1. Heat water to 95F. Alternatively, use the hottest water from your tap.
  2. Add instant yeast to the water and stir. Let it sit at room temperature for 5 minutes.
  3. In large bowl, mix flour and salt.
  4. Add in all water and mix with the flour until a shaggy dough forms.
  5. Cover with plastic wrap and let sit for 20 minutes.
  6. Remove dough onto a work surface and knead for 20 minutes using the slap and fold method.
  7. Transfer the dough back to the bowl. Cover. Let rest 15 minutes.
  8. Slap and fold again for 2 minutes. Roll dough into taut ball.
  9. Lightly coat a bowl with olive oil.Place dough in the bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Place in fridge for 18 hours.
  10. Take dough out of fridge. Cut into 6 evenly weighed pieces. Roll into balls
  11. Gently flour a baking tray. Evenly distribute the dough balls on the tray.
  12. Cover the dough balls and let proof at room temperature for 3 hours.
  13. Add tomatoes to a large bowl using a strainer. Squeeze out all excess water.
  14. Add all tomato flesh, garlic, and a splash of olive oil to a food processor. Process until smooth.
  15. Add salt to your liking. Blend together.
  16. When dough is ready, pre-heat oven to 450F.
  17. Roll out dough into thin, 10"rounds. Place onto a round baking sheet.
  18. Add sauce, basil, and mozzarella as per your liking to the dough.
  19. Cook on the top rack for around 15 minutes until the bottom is crisp and the cheese is bubbling.

    20. Enjoy!
