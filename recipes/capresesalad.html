Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad

Description

This light and easy to make meal is a showstopper. It is best served as an appetizer or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups balsamic vinegar
  • 3 whole ripe tomatoes
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, ball
  • fresh basil
  • kosher salt, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, bring balsamic vinegar to boil over medium-low heat.
  2. Let it cook for ten to twenty minutes until it has reduced in volume and has become thicker. Remove from heat and let it cool
  3. Cut the tomatoes and cheese into thick slices, about one cm thick.
  4. Arrange small bites that consist of a tomato and cheese slice. Add a basil leaf.
  5. Drizzle with the balsamic reduction.
  6. Garnish with salt and pepper to your liking.

    7. Enjoy!
